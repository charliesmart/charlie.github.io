About me

I work as news director at WHUS Radio at the University of Connecticut, where I'm currently an undergraduate student studying journalism, statistics, and design. I also produce a bi-weekly podcast, which you can check out below. Last summer I interned at WNPR public radio in Hartford, and this summer I'll be interning at TrendCT.
